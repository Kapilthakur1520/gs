Kapil Thakur

Researcher | Geospatial Engineer | 3D Reconstruction | GIS & Remote Sensing

A passionate Researcher and Geospatial Engineer currently pursuing my Master's degree in Civil Engineering at IIT Roorkee. My research focuses on "3D Reconstruction Using Deep Learning Techniques and Novel View Synthesis", where I integrate data from LiDAR, photogrammetry, drone imagery, and 360-degree videos to create high-quality 3D models.

I hold a B.Tech in Civil Engineering from NIT Hamirpur, and I previously worked as a Civil Engineer at SJVN, contributing to critical infrastructure projects. Over the years, I have honed skills in GIS tools like QGIS and ArcGIS, drone data processing, LiDAR, photogrammetry, and programming in Python and MATLAB.

My passion lies in applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques to solve complex geospatial challenges and push the boundaries of 3D modeling technology.
